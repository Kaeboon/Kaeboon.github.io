Robomaster Mechanical Design

Oct 2020 - Dec 2021

Project Description:
● Led a team of 5 students to design and build two mobile robots from scratch to completion, secured a commendable fourth-place finish in the official DJI RoboMaster 2021 outting.
● Designed and modified over 100 mechanical components, including bars, rods, and mounts for the robot's chassis, gimbal, and claw, and produced detailed engineering drawings using SolidWorks, in adherence to engineering principles such as GD&T, DFM and DFA.
● Managed procurement of mechanical parts, liaised with the fabrication lab for in-house production of sheet metals, bars, and CNC-fabricated components, ensuring strict adherence to the project budget.
● Worked closely with the electrical and software students to assemble and integrate the overall system, conducted tests to analyze the two robots’ overall feasibility, design, operation and performance.

Knowledge Demonstrated:
Mechanical Design, Manufacturing Processes, Product Development

Skillset Demonstrated:
Solidworks, GD&T, DFM, DFA, FEA
