Tilted Twister Rubiks Cube Solving Robot

Project Description:
My personal hobby project done when I was in primary school. Involving a color sensor to scan the 6 surfaces of the Rubik's Cube, and 3 motors to turn, fix and rotate the cube. I took reference from "Tilted Twister" Open Source design.
