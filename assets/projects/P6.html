AI-powered Chess Game

Aug 2022 - Nov 2022

Project Description:
Project for NUS CS3243: Introduction to Artificial Intelligence. In this project, I implemented an Alpha-Beta Pruning Algorithm to strategize a winning move against the player. I also implemented the graphical interface for easier visualization of the chessboard.
