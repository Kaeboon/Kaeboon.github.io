Line-follower Robot

May 2021 - Aug 2021

Project Description:
Project for ME2104, where I developed a simple line-following algorithm to ensure the robot stays on the path of the black line. The robot consists of two LDR sensors to detect the brightness of the ground. Simple if-else statements were used.

Knowledge Demonstrated:
Mechatronics

Skillset Demonstrated:
Arduino, Logic Gate
