Bluetooth-controlled Arduino Robot Car

May 2021 - Aug 2021

Project Description:
Project for EG1311: Design & Make, where I designed a small-scale Arduino robot car to navigate rough terrains.

Some additional features include:
LED that lights up when obstacles are detected.
Light strip that lights up when the robot is inside a dark environment as detected by the LDR sensor.
Bluetooth connection to a mobile phone, allowing remote control of the robot's motion.
Mobile phone application consisting of buttons allowing the user to control the movement of the robot.


Knowledge Demonstrated:
Mechatronics, Android App development

Skillset Demonstrated:
Arduino, Circuit Design, Logic Gate, MIT App Inventor
